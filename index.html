About Me

I am a biomedical engineer interested in the interface of immunology and biomaterials in the context of inflammation, tissue regeneration, and women's health.

My research centers around the design and development of biomaterials to manipulate the local microenvironment in fibrosis and the development of in-vitro testbeds for human diseases that cannot be replicated in animal models.
